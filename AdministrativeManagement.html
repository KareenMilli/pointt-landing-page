Administrative Management

HR System

Staff Management: Keep track of existing staff members, access and update employee information, including personal details, job roles, and performance records.
Open Positions: View a list of current job openings within the organization, add new job positions as needed, specifying job titles, and requirements.
Applicants: Manage a database of job applicants, track applicant status, and hiring decisions.
Easily add new employees to the system, input and store detailed employee information, including contact details, employment history, qualifications, and salary details.

Team

Team Management: Access team information, including goals and members' details, for clear understanding and alignment,
Activity Tracker: Monitor team activities and assign tasks efficiently to ensure project milestones are met.
Attendance and Incidents: Keep track of team attendance and address any incidents or issues that may arise promptly. By monitoring attendance patterns and addressing incidents in a timely manner, this feature helps maintain a positive and productive work environment. Additionally, it facilitates transparency and accountability within the team.

Asset

Inventory Management: Keep tabs on machines and materials., and add new items. It ensures that the organization has a clear overview of its assets.
Supplier Management: Maintain supplier details, allowing for easy reference and communication. New suppliers can be added, streamlining the procurement process.
Order Management: Monitor asset movement, including incoming and outgoing orders as well as requested items. It provides visibility into asset transactions and helps ensure that the organization has the necessary resources needs.

Transportation


Scheduled Transport: Coordinate school bus schedules and routes.
Private Transport: Manage individual student transportation arrangements.
Shared Transport: Organize group transportation options.
Incident Transport: This transportation management system ensures safe, efficient, and reliable transportation optionsf.
