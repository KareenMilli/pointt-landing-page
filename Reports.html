Reports

Feedback Reports

Feedback Collection: Aggregates feedback from students, parents, and staff, providing a holistic view of experiences and opinions.
Sentiment Analysis: Analyzes feedback sentiment to gauge overall satisfaction and identify areas needing attention.
Actionable Insights: Provides actionable insights from feedback data, guiding improvements and decision-making.

Productivity Reports


Student Productivity: Tracks student productivity metrics, identifying high performers and those needing support.
Staff Productivity: Monitors staff productivity, helping to optimize workforce management and improve efficiency.
Performance Metrics: Provides detailed performance metrics for both students and staff, aiding in overall productivity enhancement.

General Tracking Reports

Activity Monitoring: Tracks general activities and participation, giving a broad view of engagement and involvement.
Behavioral Insights: Offers insights into student behavior patterns, helping to identify and support at-risk students.
Engagement Metrics: Measures student engagement through various metrics, aiding in improving overall educational experience.

Grading Reports

Grade Distribution: Provides an overview of grade distribution across subjects and classes, highlighting academic performance trends.
Individual Progress: Tracks individual student progress, offering insights into strengths and areas for improvement.
Comparison Analysis: Allows comparison of grades across different periods, subjects, and student groups for deeper analysis.

Incident Reports


Incident Logging: Documents incidents comprehensively, providing details and context for each event.
Resolution Tracking: Tracks the resolution process of incidents, ensuring proper follow-up and closure.
Trend Analysis: Analyzes incident trends over time, helping to identify recurring issues and implement preventive measures.

Attendance Reports

Comprehensive Tracking: Includes detailed attendance reports for students, bus services, and staff, ensuring all aspects are covered.
Daily, Weekly, Monthly Views: Offers various time frames for viewing attendance data, allowing for flexible analysis.
Generates alerts for significant absenteeism, helping to identify and address attendance issues promptly.

Inventory Reports


Inventory Levels: Monitors current inventory levels, ensuring adequate supply of materials and resources.
Usage Reports: Tracks usage patterns of inventory items, helping to manage and predict future needs.
Stock Alerts: Generates alerts for low stock levels, ensuring timely replenishment and avoiding shortages.

Financial Reports


Revenue Forecast: Projects future revenue based on current data, aiding in financial planning and budgeting.
Summarizes financial performance, highlighting profit and loss over a specified period.
Offers a detailed analysis of financial health, helping to make informed financial decisions.
