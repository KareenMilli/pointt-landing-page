Interactive Module

Request System

Streamlined Requests: Allows users to submit various requests (e.g., IT support, maintenance) efficiently.
Tracking: Tracks the status of submitted requests in real-time, providing updates and ensuring transparency.
Prioritization: Helps prioritize requests based on urgency and importance, ensuring critical issues are addressed promptly.

Announcements

Centralized Announcements: Broadcasts important messages and updates to all users from a central platform.
Scheduling: Allows scheduling of announcements for future dates, ensuring timely communication.
Audience Targeting: Targets specific user groups with relevant announcements, enhancing communication efficiency.

Email System


Integrated Email: Provides an integrated email system for seamless internal and external communication.
Notifications: Sends email notifications for important updates and alerts, ensuring users stay informed.
Email Templates: Offers customizable email templates for consistent and professional communication.

Highlight System


Highlight Achievements: Recognizes and highlights individual and team achievements, boosting morale and motivation.
Sharing Success: Shares success stories and milestones, fostering a culture of appreciation and recognition.
Public Display: Displays highlights on the main dashboard for visibility and celebration by the entire organization.

Notify System


Instant Notifications: Delivers instant notifications for important events, updates, and reminders to keep users informed.
Multi-Channel Alerts: Supports notifications via email, SMS, and push notifications, ensuring broad reach.
Customizable Alerts: Allows users to customize their notification preferences for personalized information delivery.

Feedback System


Enables users to provide feedback on various aspects, such as services, events, and overall experience.
Analysis: Analyzes feedback data to identify trends, strengths, and areas for improvement.
Actionable Insights: Provides actionable insights based on feedback, helping the organization make informed decisions.
